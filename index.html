FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How long does it take to build my site?
+
Most sites are live within 3–5 business days. Premium projects with booking systems or payment integrations may take up to 7 days. We'll give you an exact timeline before we start.
Do I need to write any content myself?
+
Not at all. We write all the copy for you based on a short questionnaire. If you have photos or a logo, great — send them over. If not, we source professional images that fit your brand perfectly.
What if I already have a website?
+
We can redesign and rebuild your existing site from the ground up. Tell us what isn't working — slow loading, looks outdated, not mobile-friendly — and we fix all of it.
Is there a long-term contract?
+
Never. Monthly support is completely optional and month-to-month. Cancel any time with 30 days notice. We earn your business every single month — that's the only way we like to operate.
Will my business actually show up on Google?
+
Yes. Every site we build is SEO optimized from day one — proper page titles, meta descriptions, fast loading, and mobile responsiveness. We also set up your Google My Business so you show up in local map searches.
What does the optional monthly support include?
+
Text and photo updates, security updates, uptime monitoring, and direct access to us whenever something needs to change. Think of us as your on-call web team — there when you need us.
